SAMOSA

Receipe of samosa with it
and grocery list.

Samosa is a popular triangular pastry filled with a savory mixture, typically composed of spiced potatoes, peas, and sometimes minced meat. The outer layer is made from a thin, crispy dough, creating a delightful contrast with the flavorful and aromatic interior. Deep-fried to golden perfection, samosas are enjoyed as a delicious and portable snack in many South Asian cuisines. They are often served with chutney for an extra burst of taste.

Incredients:

  1. Flour
  2. Water (required amount)
  3. Boiled Potatoes
  4. Corriander
  5. Garam Masala
  6. Chilli Powder
  7. Salt
  8. Refined oil
Receipe:
  1. Take flour and add water to it
  2. knight the flour till it form a dough and keep it in rest for 2 hours.
  3. Take mashed potatoes, add salt according to the taste,add 1 teaspoon of both garam masala and chilli powder
  4. mix it well.
  5. Now take the dough and take out small portion of it and roll it and turn around the rolled dough to form a cone shape.
  6. Fill the potatoes filling in it and close the other end
  7. Now heat oil for around 2minutes in medium flame and fry the samosas.
  8. serve it with pinch of rock salt on it.
