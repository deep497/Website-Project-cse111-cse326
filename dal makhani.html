DAL MAKHANI

Dal makhani, a flavourful lentil preparation, is a famous Punjabi delicacy prepared with Rajma and urad dal, making it a nutritious meal . It is paired well with rotis, rice or naan and is loved by all.

INGREDIENTS

cup red kidney beans
1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder
2 tablespoon butter
1/2 large onion
1/4 cup tomato puree
1/4 cup fresh cream
1/4 teaspoon ginger paste
water As required
salt As required
1 inch ginger
1/2 tablespoon refined oil
1 medium green chilli
1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
1/4 cup urad dal
1/4 teaspoon garlic paste

RECIPE

1. Soak urad, rajma overnight & pressure cook the next day, soak sabut urad and rajma overnight in three cups of water.
3. Drain it and pressure cook in 6 cups of water with salt as per taste.
4. Fry the onions, green chillies.
Take a kadhai on medium flame, then heat the oil and add the cumin seeds.
3. you can add half the ginger-garlic paste and stir for some time.
5. Then add some onions, chopped green chillies and tomato puree.
6. Fry until the mix turns golden. you to use ghee instead of oil.
5.Add the rajma & dal Once the bhuna masala is ready you can add the pressure cooked Rajma and dal, and bring to a boil.
you can grind the masala first and then add the dal and Rajma.
7. Add the garam masala & garnish with fresh cream & coriander leaves Then add garam masala and salt as per your taste.
Stir well and bring to boil, if you think that the dal is too thick, you can add some water.
8. Then add some fresh cream to this recipe and stir well.
This will make your Dal Makhani creamy and delicious. Garnish this recipe with fresh cream and freshly chopped coriander leaves.
