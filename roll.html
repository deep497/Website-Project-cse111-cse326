Incredients:
  1. Flour
  2. Water
  3. Refined oil
  4. Rock Salt
  5. Paneer Cubes (100gm)
  6. Hung curd
  7. Spices: Garam Masala, Kashmiri chilli powder, Corriander Powder
  8. 1 Sliced Lemon
  9. Chopped onions
  10. Green Chutney

Receipe:
  1. In two different bowl add flour to one and add hung curd to other
  2. Add water to flour and spices to curd and mix both well
  3. Keep the dough at rest and add paneer cubes to curd mixture and marinate it well.
  4. Shallow fry the paneer cubes till they get golden brown.
  5. Cut the dough in small pieces and roll it in circular shape.
  6. Heat a tawa and put 2 teaspoon of oil and plave the rolled dough and cook it from both sides.
  7. Now take it on plate and add panner cubes on it add green chutney, chopped onions, and squeeze a slice of lemon on it.
  8. Roll it tightly and serve it with mustard sauce.
